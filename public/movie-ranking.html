My top 3 movies recommended for all time

  • Fast and Furious

    Fast and Furious is an action-packed movie that follows a group of criminals as they engage in high-speed chases and heists.

  • Ford and Ferrari

    Ford and Ferrari is a biographical sports drama film that chronicles the rivalry between Carroll Shelby and Ken Miles as they compete in the 1960s.

  • IronMan

    IronMan is a superhero film that follows the story of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist who becomes the armored superhero
